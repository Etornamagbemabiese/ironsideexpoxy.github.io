Professional coating solutions tailored to your space, style, and budget.

Decorative Flake System

Transform your space with decorative epoxy flooring that combines beauty and durability. Perfect for garages, basements, patios, showrooms, and commercial spaces.

1. Site Assessment

We inspect the concrete to uncover cracks, imperfections, or existing issues and understand the surface condition before work begins.

2. Surface Profiling

The floor is mechanically ground using professional diamond equipment to remove contaminants and create proper texture for strong adhesion.

3. Epoxy Installation

We prepare and apply a premium epoxy system in your chosen color. Decorative flakes are added as desired to enhance performance.

4. Cure & Protective Finish

After curing, excess flakes are cleared and a high-performance polyaspartic topcoat is applied for a smooth, UV-resistant, durable finish.

Metallic Floor System

Create an artistically crafted floor designed to suit your vision. These unique finishes create an elegant and durable surface with stunning visual depth.

1. Site Assessment

Detailed evaluation of the concrete to identify cracks, moisture concerns, and surface conditions that could affect performance or appearance.

2. Preparation & Base Coat

We mechanically prepare the slab using diamond grinding, then install a solid epoxy base coat for a uniform foundation.

3. Metallic Design Application

Metallic pigments are blended to achieve the desired look, from subtle marbling to bold visual effects unique to your space.

4. Protective Top Coat

A high-quality clear topcoat seals the surface, enhances gloss, and protects against wear, chemicals, and UV exposure.

Concrete Staining

Revitalize your space with vibrant stains and a durable sealer that lasts. Bring your concrete to life with rich color, character, and long-lasting protection.

1. Site Assessment

We inspect the concrete for defects, previous coatings, and overall suitability for staining before any work begins.

2. Surface Preparation

The area is carefully cleaned and prepped to promote proper stain penetration. Repairs are completed and failing sealers removed.

3. Stain Installation

The selected stain is applied using controlled techniques ensuring balanced coverage and consistent coloration across the entire floor.

4. Sealer Application

A high-performance sealer is installed to protect the finish from abrasion, moisture, and discoloration based on your environment.

Concrete Overlays

A stylish and durable way to refresh worn surfaces. Add depth and texture to any space to give it a brand new look without full replacement.

1. Site Assessment

We inspect the concrete for defects, previous coatings, and overall suitability to ensure the overlay will bond properly.

2. Surface Preparation

The area is cleaned, prepped, and necessary repairs are completed to ensure the overlay adheres perfectly.

3. Overlay Installation

The concrete overlay is measured, applied, and finished with expert techniques for a smooth, even surface or desired texture.

4. Sealer Application

A high-performance sealer protects the overlay from wear, moisture, and discoloration for a long-lasting, flawless finish.

Service FAQs

Helpful details before you request your assessment.

How long does installation take?

Most residential floors are completed in one to two days. Larger or heavily damaged slabs may need additional prep time.

What affects project cost?

Pricing depends on square footage, existing surface condition, moisture issues, and the system you choose.

Do you handle residential and commercial jobs?

Yes. We install durable systems for garages, showrooms, retail floors, and high-traffic industrial spaces.

What should I send for a fast quote?

Share your location, estimated floor size, current concrete condition, and preferred finish style.
