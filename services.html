Our Services

Professional coating solutions for your space.

Decorative Flake System

Transform your space with decorative epoxy flooring that combines beauty and durability. Perfect for garages, basements, patios, showrooms, and commercial spaces.

1. Site Assessment

We begin by inspecting the concrete to uncover cracks, imperfections, or existing issues and to understand the condition of the surface before work begins.

2. Surface Profiling

The floor is mechanically ground using professional diamond equipment to remove contaminants and create the proper texture needed for strong epoxy adhesion.

3. Epoxy Installation

Next, we prepare and apply a premium epoxy system in your chosen color. Decorative flakes can be added as desired to enhance performance.

4. Cure & Protective Finish

After the coating has cured, excess flakes are cleared and a high-performance clear polyaspartic topcoat is applied, delivering a smooth, UV resistant, and durable finish designed for long-term protection.

Metallic Floor System

Create an artistically crafted floor, designed to suit your endeavors. These unique finishes create an elegant and durable surface.

1. Site Assessment

Starting with a detailed evaluation of the concrete to identify cracks, moisture concerns, and surface conditions that could affect performance or appearance.

2. Preparation & Base Coat

We mechanically prepare the slab using diamond grinding to achieve the proper profile, then install a solid epoxy base coat that provides a uniform foundation for the metallic system.

3. Metallic Design Application

Once the base is set, metallic pigments are blended to achieve the desired look, from subtle marbling to bold visual effects.

4. Protective Top Coat

After the metallic layer has cured, a high-quality clear topcoat is applied to seal the surface, enhance gloss, and protect against wear, chemicals, and UV exposure, ensuring long-lasting beauty and durability.

Concrete Staining

Revitalize your space in a way that makes sense. Upgrade any concrete surface with vibrant stains and a durable sealer that lasts. Bring your concrete to life with rich stains that add color, character, and long-lasting protection.

1. Site Assessment

We start by inspecting the concrete to check for defects, previous coatings, and overall suitability for staining before any work begins.

2. Surface Preparation

The area is carefully cleaned and prepped to promote proper stain penetration and bonding. Required repairs are completed, and any compromised or failing sealers are removed using appropriate solvent treatments.

3. Stain Installation

With the surface properly prepared, the selected stain is applied using controlled techniques that ensure balanced coverage and consistent coloration across the entire floor.

4. Sealer Application

Once the stain has fully set, a high-performance concrete sealer is installed to protect the finish from abrasion, moisture, and discoloration. The sealer is chosen based on the environment and the preferred sheen level.

Concrete Overlays

Implement a stylish and durable way to refresh boring surfaces. Add depth and texture to any space to give it a brand new look.

1. Site Assessment

We inspect the concrete for defects, previous coatings, and overall suitability to ensure the overlay will bond properly.

2. Surface Preparation

The area is cleaned, prepped, and any necessary repairs are completed. This ensures the overlay adheres perfectly.

3. Overlay Installation

The concrete overlay is measured, applied, and finished with expert techniques to achieve a smooth, even surface or desired texture.

4. Sealer Application

A high-performance sealer is applied to protect the overlay from wear, moisture, and discoloration, ensuring a long-lasting, flawless finish.
