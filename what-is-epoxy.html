Understanding Options

Epoxy vs. Polyaspartic

Pure Epoxy Coatings

Durable, two-part coating that creates a thick, protective layer on concrete by binding to the concrete creating a seal.

  • Excellent durability and adhesion
  • Chemical and stain resistant
  • Takes longer to cure depending on the size of the project
  • More expensive

Polyaspartic Coatings

Polyaspartic is a combination of polyurea and epoxy, producing a new stronger, yet cost effective chemical which has the protective capacity of epoxy with added benefits from the polyurea.

  • Faster cure times
  • UV stable - won't yellow
  • Extremely durable and flexible
  • Can apply in various temperatures

Perfect for Garages

  • Protects from oil, chemicals, and moisture
  • Easy-to-clean, slip-resistant surface
  • Dramatically improves appearance
  • Resists hot tire pickup and impacts
